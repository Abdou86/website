What We Do

We provides a complete range of sustainable agricultural and export solutions

Export and international trade of agricultural and industrial products

We source and export agricultural products and industrial raw materials from Algeria and selected partner regions, supplying international buyers with consistent quality, competitive pricing, and reliable volumes under agreed Incoterms.

Quality control, product specifications, and compliance documentation

All products are handled according to defined technical specifications and quality requirements. We provide complete documentation such as TDS, SDS, certificates of origin, and compliance documents to meet international regulatory and buyer standards.

Bulk supply, packaging, and logistics coordination

We manage bulk supply operations, packaging options, and end-to-end logistics coordination, including port handling, containerization, and shipment scheduling to ensure timely and secure delivery.

Long-term sourcing partnerships for global buyers

We build long-term partnerships with producers, processors, and international buyers, focusing on stable supply contracts, transparency, and sustainable trade relationships.

Recent Works

We love what we do, check out some of our latest works

Low & Zero-Calorie Natural Sweetening

Pure sweetness from nature not artificial.

Our Low & zero-calorie natural sweeteners come from plant-based sources and provide intense sweetness without calories or blood sugar impact. They offer a clean taste, high stability, and seamless use in sugar-free and reduced-calorie food and beverage formulations.

Saffron

Premium Certified ISO 3632

Only saffron that meets defined purity, moisture, and chemical limits can be certified under ISO 3632, with Category "I" representing the highest quality.

High-Performance Bio-Fertilizer Production

Black Soldier Fly & vermicompost combines two powerful organic fertilizers

BSF frass and worm castings, creating a nutrient-rich soil amendment that improves soil structure, water retention, and plant immunity while managing waste.

In Vitro Tissue Culture

Advanced plant propagation under sterile laboratory conditions for healthy, uniform crops.

Rapid, disease-free plant multiplication using cutting-edge tissue culture techniques.

High-Performance Bio-Fodder Solutions

Insect-Based Feed (IBF) BSFL meal & Vermimeal

Sustainable production of premium Processed Animal Protein (PAP) through the bioconversion of organic waste using Black Soldier Fly and earthworms, providing nutrient-dense, high-quality feed ingredients for modern animal nutrition.

Mycoremediation & habitat restoration

Leveraging fungi to remediate soil and water while establishing thriving pollinator habitats

Through the use of specialized fungi, contaminants such as pesticides and heavy metals are broken down, enhancing soil health and nutrient availability. This creates optimal conditions for planting nectar- and pollen-rich species and supporting natural nesting sites, seamlessly integrating habitat restoration with mycoremediation. The result is healthier pollinator populations, reduced reliance on artificial feeding, and stronger biodiversity across the ecosystem.
